An analysis of Australian natural disasters

Length of natural disasters

Droughts and heatwaves were disasters that lasted the longest with a median value of 89 days followed by bushfires lasting a median of 11 days. Floods lasted 5 days and cyclones lasted for 2 days on median. Severe storms had a median of 0 days, meaning they only occured over a period of time shorter than one day.
